Provenance DApp

This dApp enables you to secure a hash (like a fingerprint) of a document on the blockchain. In future, you can prove that the document hasn't been changed or tampered with by generating a hash and comparing that with the hash stored on the blockchain. You could use this dApp to prove that you created something at a particular time (e.g. copyright/patent) or to prove that a reference hasn't been altered.


Enter your data. Generate the hash. Then click Store Hash and confirm the transaction with your Web3 provider. This hash is now stored on the blockchain. Make a note of the Tx Id.
